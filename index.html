About Me

I am a PhD student specializing in computational mathematics with a focus on mathematical analysis and algorithm development. My research interests lie at the intersection of theoretical mathematics and practical computational applications.

My work involves developing and analyzing algorithms for complex mathematical problems, with particular emphasis on numerical methods, optimization techniques, and their applications in various fields of science and engineering.

Research

Numerical Methods for Differential Equations

Developing efficient numerical schemes for solving partial differential equations with applications in physics and engineering. Focus on stability analysis and convergence properties.

$$\frac{\partial u}{\partial t} = \alpha \nabla^2 u + f(x,t)$$

Optimization Algorithms

Research on convergence analysis of optimization algorithms, particularly for non-convex problems and large-scale applications.

$$\min_{x \in \mathbb{R}^n} f(x) \text{ subject to } g(x) \leq 0$$

Computational Complexity Analysis

Analyzing the computational complexity of mathematical algorithms and developing more efficient approaches for solving large-scale problems.
